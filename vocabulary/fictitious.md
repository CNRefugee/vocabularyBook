# fictitious
### Pronunciation
/fɪkˈtɪʃəs/
### Definition
1. **Adjective**: Not real or true; imaginary or fabricated.
### Translation
虚构的, 假的
### Examples
1. The characters in the book are entirely fictitious.
2. She gave a fictitious name to the police.
