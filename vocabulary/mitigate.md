# mitigate
### Pronunciation
/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: To make less severe, serious, or painful.
### Translation
减轻, 缓和
### Examples
1. Steps were taken to mitigate the effects of the crisis.
2. They tried to mitigate the risks associated with the project.
