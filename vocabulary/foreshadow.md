# foreshadow
### Pronunciation
/fɔːrˈʃædoʊ/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: To indicate or suggest something that will happen in the future.
### Translation
预示，预兆
### Examples
1. The dark clouds foreshadowed the coming storm.
2. Her early interest in biology foreshadowed her future career as a scientist.
