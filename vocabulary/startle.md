# startle
### Pronunciation
/ˈstɑːrtl/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: To surprise or frighten someone suddenly, causing them to make a quick movement.
### Translation
使吃惊，使惊跳
### Examples
1. The sudden loud noise startled the children.
2. She was startled by his unexpected appearance at the door.
