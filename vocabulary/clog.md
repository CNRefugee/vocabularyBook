# clog
### Pronunciation
/klɔɡ/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: 1. To block or become blocked, preventing movement or flow.
2. **Noun**: 1. A blockage or obstruction.
### Translation
堵塞
### Examples
1. The drain was clogged with hair and soap scum.
2. She used a solution to unclog the sink.
