# inhale
### Pronunciation
/ɪnˈheɪl/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: To breathe in air, gas, smoke, etc.
### Translation
吸入, 吸气
### Examples
1. She inhaled deeply before starting her speech.
2. He accidentally inhaled some smoke from the fire.
