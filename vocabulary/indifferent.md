# indifferent
### Pronunciation
/ɪnˈdɪfrənt/
### Definition
1. **Adjective**: Having no particular interest or sympathy; unconcerned.
### Translation
冷漠的, 不关心的
### Examples
1. She was indifferent to the outcome of the game.
2. The manager seemed indifferent to the complaints of his employees.
