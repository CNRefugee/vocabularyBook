# adorn
### Pronunciation
/əˈdɔːrn/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: To make more beautiful or attractive by adding something decorative.
### Translation
装饰, 点缀
### Examples
1. They adorned the hall with flowers for the wedding.
2. Her dress was adorned with delicate lace and beads.
