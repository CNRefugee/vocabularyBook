# resign
### Pronunciation
/rɪˈzaɪn/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: To give up one's office or position.
### Translation
辞职, 放弃
### Examples
1. She decided to resign from her position as CEO.
2. After the scandal, he had no choice but to resign.
