# brat
### Pronunciation
/bræt/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: 1. A child, often used in a derogatory way to refer to a spoiled or rude child.
### Translation
小孩子；调皮的孩子
### Examples
1. He's such a brat; he always wants his way.
2. The brat threw a tantrum when he didn't get the toy.
