# shred
### Pronunciation
/ʃrɛd/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: A small strip or amount of something.
2. **Verb**: To tear or cut into small pieces.
### Translation
碎片, 少量
### Examples
1. He tore the letter into shreds.
2. I don't have a shred of doubt about it.
