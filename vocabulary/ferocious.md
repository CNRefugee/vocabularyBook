# ferocious
### Pronunciation
/fəˈrəʊʃəs/
### Definition
1. **Adjective**: Savagely fierce, cruel, or violent.
### Translation
凶猛的, 残忍的
### Examples
1. The ferocious lion attacked its prey.
2. The storm was ferocious, causing widespread damage.
