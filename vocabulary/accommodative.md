# accommodative
### Pronunciation
/əˈkɒmədəˌtɪv/
### Definition
1. **Adjective**: 1. Willing to adjust to others' needs; flexible in approach.
### Translation
适应性的；通融的
### Examples
1. The central bank's accommodative policy supported economic growth.
2. She was very accommodative to the needs of her team.
