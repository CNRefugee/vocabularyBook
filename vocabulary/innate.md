# innate
### Pronunciation
/ɪˈneɪt/
### Definition
1. **Adjective**: Inborn; natural; existing in one from birth.
### Translation
天生的, 固有的
### Examples
1. He has an innate talent for music.
2. The ability to speak is an innate human characteristic.
