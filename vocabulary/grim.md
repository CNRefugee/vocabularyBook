# grim
### Pronunciation
/ɡrɪm/
### Definition
1. **Adjective**: Unpleasant or shocking to see or think about; serious or gloomy.
### Translation
严酷的, 令人沮丧的
### Examples
1. The news of the disaster was grim.
2. He looked at her with a grim expression.
