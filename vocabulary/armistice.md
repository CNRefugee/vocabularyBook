# armistice
### Pronunciation
/ˈɑːrmɪstɪs/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: 1. An agreement between warring parties to stop fighting temporarily.
### Translation
停战协议
### Examples
1. The armistice was signed after months of negotiation.
2. Both sides agreed to an armistice to allow for peace talks.
