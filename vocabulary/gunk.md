# gunk
### Pronunciation
/ɡʌŋk/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: 1. A thick, sticky, or dirty substance; mess.
### Translation
污垢；粘稠物
### Examples
1. There was a lot of gunk built up in the corners of the room.
2. The car's engine was covered in greasy gunk.
