# defy
### Pronunciation
/dɪˈfaɪ/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: 2. To challenge or confront something with a sense of boldness.
### Translation
反抗, 蔑视, 挑战
### Examples
1. The protesters defied the government's orders.
2. She defied all expectations by succeeding in the task.
