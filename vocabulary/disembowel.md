# disembowel
### Pronunciation
/ˌdɪsɪmˈbaʊəl/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: To cut open and remove the internal organs of.
### Translation
取出内脏, 剖腹
### Examples
1. The hunter disemboweled the deer after the kill.
2. In ancient times, disembowelment was a form of execution.
