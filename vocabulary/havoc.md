# havoc
### Pronunciation
/ˈhævək/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: Widespread destruction, damage, or disorder; chaos.
### Translation
大破坏，浩劫
### Examples
1. The hurricane wreaked havoc on the coastal towns.
2. Computer viruses can wreak havoc on a company's network.

- Noun

### Further Explanation
Often used with 'wreak' or 'play' (e.g., 'wreak havoc', 'play havoc'), the term emphasizes severe disruption or damage. It's used in various contexts, from natural disasters to personal life disruptions, conveying a sense of widespread and often uncontrollable negative impact.
