# stifle
### Pronunciation
/ˈstaɪfl/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: 1. To suppress or hold back; to prevent from developing freely.
### Translation
抑制；压制
### Examples
1. Her creativity was stifled by strict rules.
2. The company stifled competition by acquiring smaller businesses.
