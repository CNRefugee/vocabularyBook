# annihilate
### Pronunciation
/əˈnaɪəˌleɪt/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: 1. To destroy completely or wipe out. 2. To defeat overwhelmingly in a competition or conflict.
### Translation
彻底摧毁, 歼灭
### Examples
1. The explosion annihilated the entire city.
2. Their team was annihilated in the championship game.

### Further Explanation
The term 'annihilate' suggests total destruction or eradication, often in a violent or overwhelming manner. It can be used in both literal and metaphorical contexts.
