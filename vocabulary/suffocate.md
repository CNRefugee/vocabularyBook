# suffocate
### Pronunciation
/ˈsʌfəkeɪt/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: 1. To deprive of air or oxygen; to choke.
### Translation
窒息；使窒息
### Examples
1. The smoke began to suffocate everyone in the room.
2. He felt suffocated by the intense pressure of expectations.
