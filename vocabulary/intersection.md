# intersection
### Pronunciation
/ˌɪntəˈsɛkʃən/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: A point or line common to lines or surfaces that intersect.
### Translation
交叉点, 交集
### Examples
1. The accident happened at a busy intersection.
2. There's a traffic light at the intersection of two main roads.
