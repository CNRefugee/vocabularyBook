# divert
### Pronunciation
/daɪˈvɜːt/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: 1. To cause something or someone to change direction or focus.
### Translation
转移；使分心
### Examples
1. The police diverted traffic to avoid the accident site.
2. She tried to divert attention from her mistake.
