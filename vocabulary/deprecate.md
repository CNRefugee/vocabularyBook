# deprecate
### Pronunciation
/ˈdɛprɪkeɪt/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: 1. To express disapproval of something; to belittle or disparage.
### Translation
贬低；反对
### Examples
1. She deprecated his casual attitude toward the project.
2. Many experts deprecated the use of outdated methods.
