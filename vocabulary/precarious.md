# precarious
### Pronunciation
/prɪˈkɛəriəs/
### Definition
1. **Adjective**: Not securely held or in position; dangerously likely to fall or collapse.
### Translation
不稳定的, 危险的
### Examples
1. The ladder was in a precarious position.
2. She made a precarious living as a freelance writer.

- Adjective
