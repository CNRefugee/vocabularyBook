# stagnation
### Pronunciation
/stæɡˈneɪʃən/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: 1. The state of not moving or flowing, leading to lack of development or progress.
### Translation
停滞
### Examples
1. Economic stagnation is a concern for policymakers.
2. There was a period of cultural stagnation in the country.
