# jeopardy
### Pronunciation
/ˈdʒɛpərdi/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: Danger of loss, harm, or failure.
### Translation
危险, 风险
### Examples
1. The company's future is in jeopardy.
2. He was in jeopardy of losing his job.
