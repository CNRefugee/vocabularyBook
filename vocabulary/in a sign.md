# in a sign
### Pronunciation
/ɪn ə saɪn/
### Definition
1. **Phrase**: 1. Indicating a particular development or change.
### Translation
预示着；表明
### Examples
1. In a sign of recovery, the economy grew by 2%.
2. In a sign of trust, he gave her access to his personal files.
