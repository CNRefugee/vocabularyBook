# imbue
### Pronunciation
/ɪmˈbjuː/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: To inspire or permeate with a feeling or quality.
### Translation
渗透，灌输
### Examples
1. The teacher tried to imbue her students with a love of learning.
2. His speeches imbue the audience with hope and optimism.
