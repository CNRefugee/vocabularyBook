# comply
### Pronunciation
/kəmˈplaɪ/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: 1. To act in accordance with rules, standards, or requests.
### Translation
遵守, 服从
### Examples
1. All companies must comply with the new safety regulations.
2. He complied with his parents' request to clean his room.
