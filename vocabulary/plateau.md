# plateau
### Pronunciation
/ˈplætəʊ/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: An area of relatively level high ground; a state of little or no change following a period of activity or progress.
### Translation
高原, 稳定期
### Examples
1. The hikers reached the plateau by midday.
2. Her career has reached a plateau.
