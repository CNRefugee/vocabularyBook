# mortuary
### Pronunciation
/ˈmɔːtjʊəri/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: A place where bodies are kept, especially to be identified or prepared for burial.
### Translation
停尸间, 殡仪馆
### Examples
1. The hospital's mortuary is located in the basement.
2. The funeral home has a small mortuary.
