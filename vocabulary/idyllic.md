# idyllic
### Pronunciation
/ɪˈdɪlɪk/
### Definition
1. **Adjective**: Extremely happy, peaceful, or picturesque.
### Translation
田园诗般的, 悠闲的
### Examples
1. They enjoyed an idyllic vacation on a remote island.
2. The village was an idyllic place to grow up.
