# awe
### Pronunciation
/ɔː/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: A feeling of reverential respect mixed with fear or wonder.
2. **Verb**: To inspire with awe.
### Translation
敬畏, 惊叹
### Examples
1. The sight of the Grand Canyon filled me with awe.
2. She was awed by the sheer scale of the project.
