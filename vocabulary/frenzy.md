# frenzy
### Pronunciation
/ˈfrɛnzi/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: A state or period of uncontrolled excitement or wild behavior.
### Translation
狂暴, 狂乱
### Examples
1. The crowd was in a frenzy over the band's performance.
2. She worked herself into a frenzy trying to finish the project.
