# futile
### Pronunciation
/ˈfjuːtaɪl/
### Definition
1. **Adjective**: Incapable of producing any useful result; pointless.
### Translation
徒劳的, 无效的
### Examples
1. Their efforts to save the company were futile.
2. It was futile to continue the argument.
