# supply glut
### Pronunciation
/səˈplaɪ ɡlʌt/
### Definition
1. **Noun**: 1. An excessive supply of a product, leading to lower prices and reduced profitability.
### Translation
供应过剩
### Examples
1. The oil industry is facing a supply glut.
2. A supply glut of smartphones has driven prices down.

- Noun
