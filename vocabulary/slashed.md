# slashed
### Pronunciation
/slæʃt/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: 1. To reduce something dramatically or severely.
### Translation
削减的；大幅减少的
### Examples
1. The company slashed its prices to boost sales.
2. Funding for the project was slashed by 50%.
