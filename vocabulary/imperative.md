# imperative
### Pronunciation
/ɪmˈpɛrətɪv/
### Definition
1. **Adjective**: 1. Of vital importance; crucial.
2. **Noun**: 1. A command or order.
### Translation
必要的；紧急的
### Examples
1. It is imperative that we address this issue immediately.
2. The doctor gave an imperative to rest.
