# shatter
### Pronunciation
/ˈʃætər/
### Definition
1. **Verb**: To break suddenly into many small pieces.
### Translation
粉碎, 破碎
### Examples
1. The glass vase fell and shattered into a thousand pieces.
2. The news shattered her hopes.
